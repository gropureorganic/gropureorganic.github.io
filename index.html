Who We Are

We are a group of driven individuals passionate about creating sustainable organic produce.

Gropure organic was started with the mission of bringing organically grown, chemical and pesticide free food in your kitchens. We believe in providing food that nourishes the soul and is nutrient rich. We are committed to organic purity, integrity of our purpose and following ethical practices as pure food is a right of every human.
Our food is sustainably grown with love by our farmers because we care about your health and the health of our planet.

What We Do

We have the skills and the drive to empower people with Clean and responsible food.

All food products are grown in our farmlands by our farmers because we believe in transparency, traceability and accountability.

Organically grown

Our brand stands for pure, organically grown food with no use of pesticides and fertilisers. Gropure’s food is completely residue, chemical free and grown with Non-GMO seeds.

Helping marginal farmers

We are committed to our farmer friends by training them to grow organic food, engage farmer families, deliver better returns and help create a sustainable livelihood for them. Farmers earn more, their children go to school and the love is returned in the farming.

Sustainable farming (circle of life)

Our farms are self-sustainable and the resources required in farming are created within the farms. We do water harvesting and use groundwater. Apply natural solutions to control pests, use biofertilizers and decomposers made on the farm by our farmers. A commitment to make it a win- win for the farmer, consumer and the environment.

Team of specialists

Gropure has specialists with over 20 years of experience in organic farming, veterinary to support dairy farming and farm managers to manage each of our farmland.

Minimal processing

We do minimal processing to preserve the nutritional values, colour and taste of food ensuring a product 100% safe to consume. We apply age – old proven practices of cultivation, farming, harvesting and storage.

Farm to fork

We pay attention to every detail from farm to your doorstep. Grown in our farms in the right weather, right soil by the right farmer. Absence of middlemen. Grown by us, sold by us.
